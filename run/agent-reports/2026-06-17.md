**2. Long-form script opening hook** (`app/services/engines/worker.py`)  
Added explicit hook guidance to the long-form script prompt: open with a direct, punchy 2-3 sentence hook that addresses the tension/question in the title; forbidden openers: "In this video", "Today we will", "Welcome back". Motivated by the retention outlier (Kubernetes at 91.7% vs. 43-48% for others) — the doubt-frame title likely sets a viewer expectation that the video immediately addresses.
