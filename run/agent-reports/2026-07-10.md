**Targeted: R4 pacing → shipped (2 changes, both `app/services/engines/storyboard.py`).**
Root cause: beat durations are purely emergent — the LLM anchors each beat with a 2-6-word `cue`
and a beat runs until the next cue, but nothing told the LLM to SPACE cues; `align_storyboard`
only floors beats at `_MIN_DUR=0.5s`.
1. **Prompt:** new CRITICAL RULE 6 (PACING) — cue spacing IS screen time; one beat per ~8-14
   words; hook = first sentence only; no gap >~25 words; diagram/compare/code ≥~10 words; plan
   the ending backwards (cta ~8-10 words before end, payoff the ~10 before that).
2. **Code:** `_TAIL_MIN=2.0` tail floor in `align_storyboard` — prompt alone failed on the tail
   (2 renders: LLM kept anchoring quote+cta on the script's final words, 0.85s/0.97s, because the
   quotable line IS the last sentence). The floor pulls the last two beats' starts EARLIER only
   (stealing from a long predecessor, never breaking monotonicity), guarded to no-op on clips too
   short to grant it (keeps tiny/degenerate clips pure word-sync).
